aboutOkinawa

Okinawa is located in the most southern part of Japan where its weather is subtropical. Some delicious Okinawan cuisines include Goya Champloo, and soba, made from buckwheat noodles. As for sightseeing, there is a famous castle called Shuri Castle from the Ryukyu era. Another popular sight is the Makishi Public Market where you can eat fresh fish and vegitable right in the market. This market is located in the heart of Okinawan's International Street. Also, you are going to encounter the Schiesser which is known as protector of Okinawa. If you would like to see the largest whale shark, please visit Mira Aquarium. Okinawa is known for its tropical fruits such as pineapples, Jabochikaba, mango,papaya,canister,pitanga, banana, senari Banana, dragon fruit, paramounts, remb, passion fruit, supporzilla, guava, and litchi. All these fruits can found at OKINAWA fruitsland. Please visit Okinawa.
